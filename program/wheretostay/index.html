Solo Sokos Hotel Torni Tampere

Solo Sokos Hotel Torni Tampere offers an all-new hotel experience. The hotel is located in the heart of Tampere next to the railway station. Reaching a height of 88 metres, the modern hotel tower combined with the old locomotive depots provide the best possible environment to experience the enticing story of Tampere and its phenomena, from local rock icons to sports heroes.
