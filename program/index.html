Investor Pre-Event (with Investor Ticket)

Date: Monday 8th October 2018 Schedule: 18:00 - 21:00
Location: Tampere (TBD)
Entrance: Investor pass for Stream

This exclusive networking event will provide the opportunity for investors to meet prior to Stream Startup Festival event and hear more from the 2 top angel Israeli investor guests: Israel Gideon Y. Ben-Zvi and Eran Yarkoni.

Gideon Ben-Zvi is a successful serial entrepreneur who has founded six companies as CEO or Chairman, resulting in one IPO and two M&A events thus far. His other companies are all emerging market leaders in their spaces, experiencing significant growth.

No financial details were officially disclosed but according to the press, Canon paid $90 million for BriefCam, a company Gideon co-founded in 2008 that provides video synopisis and machine learning solutions.

Eran Yarkoni

Focused on startups owning a significant technological advantage.

His firm, Israel Tech Gate, provides technology scouting services to international corporations, helping them identify breakthrough technologies, potential partners, investment and M&A opportunities in Israel and north Europe.

Eran co-founded and led EnStorage, a developer of breakthrough energy storage systems for renewable and conventional energy applications, and CyOptics. CyOptics is a leading provider of high-speed active components for optical networks, which was acquired by Avago Technologies. CyOptics Exit value was about $400M.

His investment track record includes the role of CEO of Capital Nature, a renewable energy incubator, and Venture Partner in Israel Seed Partners, a $250M early stage Israeli Venture Capital fund, as well as seed investments in early stage startups.
