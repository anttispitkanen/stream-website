Panel Discussions

Have you ever been wondering, how are the startups rolling in other parts of the world? What could we learn from them?

Or would you like to hear investors discuss about VC funding?

Choose the panel discussions that inspire you the most:

1) ISRAELI STARTUP ECOSYSTEM CASE

How does Israel, a small country with 1.5 times the population of Finland, has on a per capita basis more venture capital, more startups and more tech professionals than any other nation in the world?

The key to Israel’s innovation success?

Come and listen to our panel discussion to find out.

Panelists: Will Cardwell (moderator), Gideon Ben-Zvi, Eran Yarkoni, Tamir Huberman

2) ISRAELI COMMERCIALISATION OF UNIVERSITY TECHNOLOGY: WHAT CAN FINLAND LEARN FROM THE STARTUP NATION?

Israeli academic institutions are pioneer in managing technology transfer and bringing innovations from University bench to market. Not to mention they have an exceptional track record of generating more revenue from IP sales than any other country - except US.

Panelists: Tamir Huberman, Pauli Kuosmanen, (more to be added by end of August)

3) CORPORATE AS STARTUP INVESTOR

Corporates are seeking faster growth and started realizing only internal R&D won't do it. The key to industry disruption - startups.

Panelists: Lauri Lehtovuori (moderator)

4) INVESTORS ON STAGE

VC funds pitching + discussion on the state of VC in Finland.

The development of venture capital funds is very promising in Finland but domestic VC money has seen a slow growth in Finland.

In 2017 several new VC funds launched in Finland.

Will 2018 exceed the record €349 million euros in venture capital raised by Finnish startups and growth companies last year?

Panelists: Matti Copeland (moderator)

Investor Pre-Event (with Investor Ticket)

Date: Monday 8th October 2018 Schedule: 18:00 - 21:00
Location: Tampere (TBD)
Entrance: Investor pass for Stream

This exclusive networking event will provide the opportunity for investors to meet prior to Stream Startup Festival event and hear more from the 2 top angel Israeli investor guests: Israel Gideon Y. Ben-Zvi and Eran Yarkoni.

Gideon Ben-Zvi is a successful serial entrepreneur who has founded six companies as CEO or Chairman, resulting in one IPO and two M&A events thus far. His other companies are all emerging market leaders in their spaces, experiencing significant growth.

No financial details were officially disclosed but according to the press, Canon paid $90 million for BriefCam, a company Gideon co-founded in 2008 that provides video synopisis and machine learning solutions.

Eran Yarkoni

Focused on startups owning a significant technological advantage.

His firm, Israel Tech Gate, provides technology scouting services to international corporations, helping them identify breakthrough technologies, potential partners, investment and M&A opportunities in Israel and north Europe.

Eran co-founded and led EnStorage, a developer of breakthrough energy storage systems for renewable and conventional energy applications, and CyOptics, a leading provider of high-speed active components for optical networks, which was acquired by Avago Technologies.

His investment track record includes the role of CEO of Capital Nature, a renewable energy incubator, and Venture Partner in Israel Seed Partners, a $250M early stage Israeli Venture Capital fund, as well as seed investments in early stage startups.
