Dream Team Application

Do you have what it takes to become a startup entrepreneur? Join Dream Team!

Dream Team is a competition that is organized first time in Stream Festival. It is a challenge that combines both an actual business case and pitching competition, which makes it a great way to find your way to the startup world.

We are looking for entrepreneurial minded individuals who have a strong drive to start own company but who haven’t found a team yet. During the competition you will work with like-minded people who all are also in a search of a founder team.

Selected teams will have free access to the Stream festival and workshops and winner team will also get a prize that will be announced soon! The main reason why many startups fail is the team so we will do a psychological screening to the pre-selected candidates to assure that team fits together. Teams will also get support after the competition to continue working with their own business ideas.

If you have ever dreamt of becoming an startup entrepreneur, this is your chance!

Apply here for the life-changing journey!

In Dream Team competition 3-4 teams with 4 people each are selected. Teams are formed based on psychometric evaluation and background information. Evaluation is great tool to understand strengths and development points in a team. Background information from the application form is used to match skills and interests.*

Competition starts with a kickoff event two weeks before the Stream Festival. Teams start by working with a business case given by the multinational company. The deadline for the case solution documentation is Friday 5.10 Before the deadline teams have one follow-up meeting with the case company.

Teams pitch their solutions in Stream Festival and the winners are announced in the Stream Festival main stage. The competition is judged by well known investors and business influencers together with case company representative.

Still not sure whether to apply? We strongly encourage you to give it a go! If you are not selected, you are also a more than welcome to the Stream Festival to enjoy all the program and network with startup entrepreneurs, investors and business influencers!

For the people who have great business ideas but don’t have a team yet, check Dream Team competition.

*Psychological evaluation is done by professionals who only have the access to test results. All the information from the application form is strictly confidential and accessible by selection team. Information from the forms is removed after the selection is done. Latest 31th October 2018.
