Stream Startup Festival - practical support and tools of growth for early stage startups.

One day full of workshops, speakers, fireside gatherings with investors, networking and answers to questions related to growth and succeeding in the startup world.

Workshops will provide hands-on experience in marketing, social media, funding and growth.

Concrete tools and working methods are taken back to the offices in virtual goodie bags after the event.

Stream Startup Festival has invited well-known Israeli investors and they are all available for discussions, support and networking. Come and meet them - and hear them discuss on the stage about their setbacks, challenges and experiences during their journey.

Premises of an old brick factory of Kuivaamo in Hiedanranta, Tampere offer a unique venue for this event. Evening will continue with After Party Carnival in spirit of Steampunk.

Student tickets are available for a deep dive into the startup world – inspiring speakers, startup companies, and most importantly - networking!
