What?

Tribe Tampere has brought the Tampere region startup ecosystem together and produced a sequence of great events from TribeX to Startup World Cup that was held this April 2018 in Pakkahuone. The winner of Startup World Cup pitching competition went to represent Finland in the San Franciscoâ€™s Grand Finale and competed for the US $1,000,000 prize in investment. Next in line for Tampere is the biggest startup event so far, Stream.

Stream brings the investors and startups together for an more interactive learning and coaching experience at the unique old brick factory in Hiedanranta, Tampere. This one day event is full of meaningful networking with your peers and interest groups. You can choose the right workshops to help you improve in needed areas.

Stream will pre-screen the startup attendees with a totally new kind of screening system done in cooperation with angel investors and psychologists. This will help investors to evaluate the team better and make better investment decisions. Startups can use this as a validation and furthermore see the areas which they can focus and improve.

There will be workshops on how to evaluate startups better in order to make better investments as well as how to build a successful startup team and improve your company selling processes.
